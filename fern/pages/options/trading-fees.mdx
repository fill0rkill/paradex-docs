---
## Overview

The Taker pays **4bps of underlying notional** as a taker fee.

The Maker pays **1bp of underlying notional** as the maker fee.

Note: the maker and taker fee are capped at **300%** of the trade price.

### Example 

Alice (taker) trades 2 BTC-USD-90000-C against Bob (maker). 
BTC spot price is 100000. 

Alice pays $$ 0.0004*2*100000=\$80 $$ and Bob pays $$ 0.0001*2*100000=\$20 $$
